# AI Research Framework

## Core Question
How can AI-assisted analytics help identify high-risk cybersecurity patterns and improve threat prioritization and incident response?

## Working Hypotheses
1. Vendors with repeated KEV entries create concentration risk and should be monitored separately.
2. KEV items linked to known ransomware campaigns should be prioritized above general exposure.
3. Remediation windows can be used as an operational risk indicator for patching urgency.
4. Monthly inflow trends can help security teams plan vulnerability management capacity.

## AI Assistance Used
AI was used to help frame hypotheses, define KPI ideas, and structure the analytical workflow. Final interpretation should be reviewed manually.
