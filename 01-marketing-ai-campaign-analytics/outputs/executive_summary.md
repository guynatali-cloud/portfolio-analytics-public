# Executive Summary

## Business Question
How can AI-assisted campaign analysis improve conversion performance and budget allocation across marketing channels?

## Key Findings
- **Best ROAS platform:** TikTok Ads (7.62x)
- **Weakest ROAS platform:** Google Ads (3.47x)
- **Best converting campaign type:** Search (4.78% conversion rate)

## AI-Assisted Recommendation
Using a blended score based on ROAS and conversion rate, the project recommends:
- Increase budget toward the strongest-performing platform(s)
- Reduce spend on lower-efficiency channels
- Use campaign-type performance to guide creative and targeting tests
- Keep analyst review in the loop before applying budget changes

## Suggested Narrative for Portfolio
This project shows how AI can help an analyst move faster from raw campaign data to channel-level recommendations. AI was used to structure the workflow, suggest scoring logic, and draft documentation, while the final analysis, organization, and publishing decisions remained analyst-led.
