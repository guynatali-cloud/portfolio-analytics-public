# Research Framework

## Core Question
How can AI-assisted campaign analysis improve conversion performance and budget allocation across marketing channels?

## Hypotheses
1. Platforms with higher ROAS and higher conversion rate deserve a larger share of budget.
2. Some campaign types may perform differently across platforms and should not receive equal budget.
3. AI-assisted scoring can speed up triage and highlight where analysts should investigate first.

## Success Metrics
- ROAS
- Conversion Rate
- CPA
- CPC
- Profit
- Revenue per Click

## Decision Rule
Use AI-assisted scoring as a prioritization tool, then validate decisions using business context, seasonality, and campaign objectives.
