# Tableau Dashboard Plan

## Dashboard Title
Biotech Healthcare AI Surgical Operations Dashboard

## Recommended Tabs
1. Executive Summary — KPI cards, total surgeries, avg delay, complication rate
2. AI vs Standard Comparison — side-by-side metrics
3. Department Performance — delay, satisfaction, cost by department
4. Surgeon Scorecard — ranked by satisfaction and complication rate
5. Monthly Trend — delay and complication trends over time
6. Operating Room Utilization — heatmap by room and shift

## Core KPIs
- Total Surgeries
- Average Delay (minutes)
- Complication Rate (%)
- Readmission Rate (%)
- Patient Satisfaction Score (1-10)
- OR Utilization Rate
- Average Cost per Surgery
- Duration Efficiency (actual vs scheduled)

## Recommended Filters
- Department
- Dashboard Type (AI-Assisted / Standard)
- Patient Risk Level
- Surgeon
- Shift
- Year / Month

## Data Source
- `tableau_monthly_extract.csv` (in this folder)
- For full dashboard: use `surgical_operations_cleaned.csv` from data/cleaned/
