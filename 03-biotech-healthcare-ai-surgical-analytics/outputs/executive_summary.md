# Executive Summary

## Business Question
Can an AI-powered surgical operations dashboard help physicians and surgeons reduce workload, identify bottlenecks, and improve operating room efficiency?

## Key Findings
- **AI-Assisted surgeries** show significantly lower average delays compared to Standard dashboard workflows
- **Complication rates** are lower in AI-Assisted cases across most departments
- **Patient satisfaction** scores average higher in AI-Assisted surgeries
- **Readmission rates** (30-day) are reduced when AI dashboards are used
- **Neurology and Cardiology** departments show the longest average surgery durations and highest potential for AI-driven optimization

## AI-Assisted Recommendation
Using a blended efficiency score based on delay, complication rate, satisfaction, and utilization:
- Prioritize AI dashboard expansion in departments with highest delays and complication rates
- Use surgeon-level scorecards to identify coaching opportunities
- Monitor monthly trends to validate AI dashboard ROI over time
- Keep clinical staff review in the loop before changing scheduling or staffing

## Suggested Narrative for Portfolio
This project demonstrates how AI-assisted dashboards can help hospital operations teams identify surgical bottlenecks, reduce delays, and improve patient outcomes. AI was used to structure the workflow, suggest scoring logic, and draft documentation, while the final analysis and interpretation remained analyst-led.
