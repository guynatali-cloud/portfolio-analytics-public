# Research Framework

## Core Question
Can an AI-powered surgical operations dashboard help physicians and surgeons reduce workload, identify bottlenecks, and improve operating room efficiency?

## Hypotheses
1. AI-assisted dashboards lead to shorter surgery delays compared to standard workflows.
2. Departments using AI dashboards show lower complication and readmission rates.
3. AI-assisted scheduling improves operating room utilization.
4. Patient satisfaction is higher when AI tools support surgical planning.

## Success Metrics
- Average delay minutes
- Complication rate
- Readmission rate (30-day)
- Patient satisfaction score
- OR utilization rate
- Duration efficiency (actual vs scheduled)

## Decision Rule
Use AI-assisted scoring as a prioritization tool for identifying departments and surgeons that would benefit most from expanded AI dashboard adoption.
