# Research Framework
## Core Question
Can AI-assisted fraud scoring reduce false positives while maintaining high detection coverage?
## Hypotheses
1. Blended risk scores outperform single-indicator fraud flags
2. Category-level thresholds improve precision vs. uniform cutoffs
3. Time-based patterns (hour) can enhance fraud detection
## Success Metrics
- Precision of High Risk tier
- Fraud coverage (% of total fraud caught by Medium+High)
- False positive rate reduction
## Decision Rule
Use AI scoring as triage, then validate with analyst review before intervention.
