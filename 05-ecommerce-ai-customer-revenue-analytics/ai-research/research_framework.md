# Research Framework
## Core Question
Does AI personalization drive sustainable LTV growth or just short-term conversion bumps?
## Hypotheses
1. VIP-tier customers (AI-identified) have higher LTV than low-priority ones
2. Satisfaction and spending are correlated but not identical
3. Discount-driven customers may convert but not retain
## Success Metrics
- LTV proxy by AI tier, satisfaction rate, recency patterns
