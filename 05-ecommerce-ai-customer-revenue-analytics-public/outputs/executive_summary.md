# Executive Summary

## Business Question
Does AI personalization increase long-term customer value — or only short-term conversions?

## Key Findings
- **Gold members** have the highest average spend and LTV
- **VIP tier** (AI-identified) shows significantly higher LTV than Low priority customers
- **Discount usage** correlates with higher satisfaction but not necessarily higher LTV
- **Recency matters:** Active customers (last 30 days) show stronger engagement across all tiers
- **AI personalization scoring** successfully separates high-value customers from low-value ones

## AI-Assisted Recommendation
- Focus personalization budget on VIP tier (highest ROI potential)
- Nurture tier customers are candidates for targeted campaigns to move them toward VIP
- Low priority customers need re-engagement or should receive minimal personalization investment
- Use satisfaction data alongside spend data to avoid over-indexing on revenue alone
