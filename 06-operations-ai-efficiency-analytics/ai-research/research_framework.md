# Research Framework
## Core Question
Can AI-assisted scoring detect operational bottlenecks early and improve resource allocation?
## Hypotheses
1. Late shipments concentrate in specific carriers/routes
2. AI efficiency scoring can separate high-performing from underperforming shipments
3. Transit time (TPT) is a strong predictor of delay risk
## Metrics: Late rate, on-time rate, transit time, throughput, AI score accuracy
