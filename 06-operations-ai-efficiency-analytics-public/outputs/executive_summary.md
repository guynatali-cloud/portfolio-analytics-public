# Executive Summary

## Business Question
How can AI-assisted analytics improve workflow efficiency, bottleneck detection, and resource allocation in business operations?

## Key Findings
- **Overall late rate:** 2.1% — most shipments arrive on time or early
- **Carrier V44_3 (CRF service)** shows different performance profile vs V444_0/V444_1
- **Delay severity:** 97.9% on time, 1.2% minor, 0.8% moderate, <0.1% severe
- **AI efficiency scoring** successfully identifies shipments needing attention vs high-performing ones
- **Monthly volume patterns** reveal seasonal throughput variation

## AI-Assisted Recommendation
- Focus operational improvement on "Needs improvement" tier shipments
- Monitor carrier-level performance trends to prevent bottleneck buildup
- Use delay severity classification to prioritize investigation
- Implement AI-assisted route/carrier scoring for proactive resource allocation
